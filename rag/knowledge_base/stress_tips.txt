High stress levels can negatively affect productivity and mental health.
Employees experiencing high stress should consider taking regular breaks,
reducing overtime hours, and practicing mindfulness techniques.

Medium stress indicates manageable pressure, but long working hours and
insufficient rest may increase burnout risk.

Low stress levels suggest good work-life balance. Maintaining consistent
sleep schedules, regular exercise, and healthy boundaries is recommended.

Organizations should encourage flexible work hours and wellness programs
to reduce employee stress.
